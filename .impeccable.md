## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are Chinese business buyers: brand teams, merchants, content teams, agencies, and operators who need a more controllable storyboard-to-video workflow before spending on production. They evaluate the product as a professional service/product workflow, not a toy AI demo.

### Brand Personality
Calm, premium, trustworthy. The product should feel like a modern creative operations platform with strong procurement confidence, not a flashy consumer AI tool.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light mode. Chinese-first copy. Editorial + product-grade. Strong visual proof, restrained color, polished spacing, premium but not luxurious in a wasteful way. Avoid neon, glassmorphism, obvious AI-demo tropes, overused SaaS blocks, and generic template feel.

### Design Principles
1. Show proof and deliverables early.
2. Make the workflow feel controllable and business-ready.
3. Keep the interface elegant, fast, and structured.
4. Use motion sparingly for clarity and polish, not spectacle.
5. Preserve strong hierarchy on mobile and desktop.
