# 镜片 Professional Landing Page Brief

## Product
镜片：先出分镜，再出视频。把产品卖点更稳地变成短视频样片。

## Goal
Build a premium, professional Chinese landing page that feels suitable for real business buyers, not a generic AI demo.

## Target audience
- 品牌方
- 商家
- 内容团队
- 代运营团队
- 小型创意团队

## Core message
- 先出分镜，再出视频
- 比直接文生视频更可控
- 更适合广告、产品展示、内容测试、内部提案
- 降低废片率，先确认方向再出片

## Visual direction
- Premium, calm, product-grade, high-trust
- Chinese professional market, not playful social-media style
- Less AI-demo feel, more like a serious creative workflow product
- Blend of Apple restraint, Stripe professionalism, and calm editorial clarity
- Light theme preferred
- Avoid neon gradients, glassmorphism, generic AI visuals, overdecorated cards, and obvious template feel

## Page requirements
Create a static landing page using HTML/CSS/JS only unless JS is barely needed.

Required sections:
1. Hero
2. Product workflow preview
3. Why not direct video generation
4. Core value points
5. Who it is for
6. How it works
7. Typical scenarios
8. Deliverables / what users get
9. Boundaries / what it is and is not
10. FAQ
11. Final CTA with form

## CTA
Primary CTA wording should be consistent across the page:
- 申请专业版内测

## Form
Fields:
- 你的身份
- 你想做的内容类型
- 联系方式
- 目前最想解决的问题

The form can be frontend-only for now, but must feel polished and show a proper success state after submit.
Saving to localStorage is acceptable.

## Quality bar
- Mobile-first responsive
- Premium typography and spacing
- Chinese copy throughout
- No console errors
- No dead sections
- Strong above-the-fold clarity in 3 seconds
- Must feel like a professional paid product, not an AI toy

## Deliverables
- index.html
- styles.css
- script.js (only if needed)
- README.md with local preview instructions
